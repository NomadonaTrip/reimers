About Our Ministry

We are a dedicated team of missionaries working with Youth For Christ in Manitoba, committed to sharing the love of Christ with young people across our province.

Compassionate Outreach

We meet youth where they are, offering support, guidance, and the message of God's love through meaningful relationships.

Community Building

Creating safe spaces where young people can grow in faith, build friendships, and discover their purpose in Christ.

Discipleship

Equipping youth with biblical truth and practical life skills to navigate challenges with faith and wisdom.

Our Mission in Manitoba

Since 2010, our team has been serving communities across Manitoba, from Winnipeg to remote northern areas, bringing the transformative message of Christ to young people.

  • Weekly youth gatherings in 5 communities
  • Annual summer camps serving 300+ youth
  • School mentorship programs in 15 schools
  • Leadership training for indigenous youth
